Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 11, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to QueueKeeper. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle any information collected through the QueueKeeper app.

Information We Don't Collect

QueueKeeper is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about our users. The app functions entirely on your device, and your data remains local to your device unless you choose to use iCloud sync.

Data Storage

  • All list data, including your movies, TV shows, books, and other items, is stored locally on your device
  • If you enable iCloud sync, your data will be stored in your personal iCloud account, which is governed by Apple's privacy policy
  • We do not have access to any data stored in your iCloud account

Third-Party Services

QueueKeeper interfaces with the following third-party services:

  • The Movie Database (TMDB) API for movie and TV show information
  • Google Books API for book information
  • Apple's iCloud for data synchronization (optional)

When you search for media within the app, these queries are sent directly to the respective APIs. We do not store or process these search queries on our servers.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

We do not collect analytics data or crash reports. Any crash reports are managed through Apple's built-in system and are anonymous.

Children's Privacy

QueueKeeper is not targeted towards children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal information for you to access, modify, or delete. All your app data is under your direct control.
